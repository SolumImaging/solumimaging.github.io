Ebiri Nkugba
Founder & Chief Pilot

I love to fly

Ebiri is our Chief Pilot and visionary. Not only is he an instrument rated commercial pilot, but he also holds FAA certifications as a flight instructor, airframe and powerplant technician. With 20 years of aviation experience including positions in Fortune 100 management, technical operations, and college instruction, he keeps us safe and legal in the air.

Alicia Nkugba
Administrative Coordinator

I Love Creativity

Alicia is our public relations leader and office manager. She studied Communications and Psychology at Cornerstone University, which inspired her passion for helping people succeed. She founded her own Life Coaching business, specializing in restoring torn relationships. When she isn’t organizing projects in the Solum Imaging office or speaking with clients, she can be found volunteering with the local outreach organization, MomsBloom, as she supports and advocates for new mothers.

Leviticus Cain
Chief Roboticist & Co-Founder

I'm proud to be a geek

Levi is our emerging technology specialist. While serving in the United States Army, he worked with Biometric Computer Systems which earned him a CID Colonel Coin and two Army Commendation Medals. Currently a senior at Grand Valley State University, he holds the Vice President’s Chair at the student chapter of The American Society of Mechanical Engineers. When he isn’t designing and programing robotics, you can find him at his fellowship position with The Mission Continues, helping other veterans transition back into civilian life.

Rachel Cain
Media & Marketing Manager

I love writing

Rachel is our social media and marketing leader. With a background in the nonprofit sector, she has worked with various charities and organized fundraising events. Currently a senior at Grand Valley State University, she is a member of both the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society and the National Society of Collegiate Scholars. She is also the co-founder of the student chapter of the American Civil Liberties Union.
